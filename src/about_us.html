Nepal Ski Association

Nepal Ski Association is the official federation regulating skiing within Nepal. Nepal Ski Association is founded to promote ski sports in Nepal. Winter sports are generally more common in the Himalayan regions of Nepal.

MISSION AND VISION

Mission

  • Identify, nurture, and develop the next generation of Nepali skiing talent by implementing robust grassroots programs, talent identification initiatives, and youth engagement activities
  • Strive to cultivate a culture of excellence in alpine sports by providing world-class training, resources, and support to athletes at all levels
  • Represent Nepal with pride on the international skiing stage, participating in prestigious competitions, and consistently achieving commendable results, showcasing the nation's skiing prowess
  • Position Nepal as a premier destination for winter sports tourism, attracting enthusiasts from around the world and contributing to the country's overall tourism sector

Vision

"To be a globally recognized hub for alpine excellence, fostering a passion for skiing, and promoting Nepal as a premier destination for winter sports."
